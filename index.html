Hand Analysis

What is Hand Analysis?

Imagine Tiger Woods watching your back-swing in real time and adjusting exactly what you are doing right and wrong.

Our pros will confidently break down what all players are doing & if it is the most optimal play or if there was a better option. This is real-time feedback designed to sharpen analytical thinking.

Optional Tournament

The "Bad Actor" Seminar

"Finding the Bad Actors"

A classroom-style interactive seminar focused on psychology, tells, and body language analysis. We teach your team how to spot deception, read non-verbal cues, and protect their stack.

These skills are ideal for both the boardroom and the poker table—perfect for a team dedicated to stopping fraud.

Optional Tournament

The Hybrid Protocol

Bring both styles together. Can't decide? We can combine the psychological depth of the Bad Actor seminar with the real-time thrill of Hand Analysis. Start with a seminar on spotting deception, then move to the tables to put those skills to the test in a coached tournament. The ultimate comprehensive experience.
